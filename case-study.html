🎯 The Challenge

Our client, a mid-sized manufacturing company, was drowning in paperwork. Their accounts payable team was manually processing hundreds of invoices each week, each arriving in different formats via email:

📧

Email Overload

300+ invoices received weekly through email in various formats (PDF, images, scanned documents)

📋

Manual Data Entry

Staff spending 40+ hours weekly manually typing invoice details into their SAP system

⏰

Slow Approval Process

Paper-based approval workflows taking 5-7 days, causing payment delays and vendor relationship issues

❌

Human Errors

15% error rate in manual data entry leading to payment disputes and reconciliation headaches

💡 Our AI-Powered Solution

We designed an intelligent system that transforms chaotic invoice processing into a smooth, automated workflow:

1

🤖 Smart Email Processing

AI monitors the company's invoice email account 24/7, automatically identifying and extracting invoices from attachments

Uses: Computer Vision + Natural Language Processing
→
2

📖 Intelligent Document Reading

Advanced OCR technology converts any invoice format (PDF, image, scan) into machine-readable text with 99.2% accuracy

Uses: Advanced OCR + Machine Learning
→
3

🧠 Smart Data Extraction

AI identifies and extracts key information: vendor name, amount, invoice number, date, line items - regardless of invoice layout

Uses: Named Entity Recognition + Pattern Matching
→
4

✅ Automated Approval Workflow

System routes invoices to appropriate managers based on amount, department, and vendor rules. Automatic approval for pre-approved vendors under threshold

Uses: Business Rules Engine + Workflow Automation
→
5

📊 SAP Integration

Approved invoices automatically upload to SAP with all details pre-filled, triggering payment scheduling and updating financial records

Uses: API Integration + Data Mapping
