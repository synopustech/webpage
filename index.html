2.1 Personal Data

Personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, company name, and other contact details when you fill out contact forms, subscribe to communications, request information, or engage with us.

2.2 Usage Data

Information automatically collected: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, clickstream data, and cookies.
